Our Story

Crafted with passion,
rooted in tradition

We believe that great food is a conversation — between land and sea, between past and present. Chef Martina Ruiz brings 20 years of experience with Michelin-starred kitchens into every plate she composes at Solera.

Our ingredients are sourced daily from local farms and fishermen, keeping our menu alive with the seasons and honest with the earth.
